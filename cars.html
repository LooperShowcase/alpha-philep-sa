Mercedes-Benz Vision AVTR

The acronym AVTR stands for advance vision transportation, but it is also a reference to the 2009 film Avatar.[4] The entire rear end of the AVTR is covered in 33 discrete scales, so called "bionic flaps" used to communicate with people outside the car. It also has special spherical wheels that were inspired by the "seeds of the Tree of Souls" from 2009's Avatar. These wheels can rotate so that the AVTR can move sideways, or even diagonally.
